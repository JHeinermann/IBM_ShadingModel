Mechanistic Shading in
Individual-based Modelling

This is a small Website to present our Individual-Based Modelling Approach of Shading.

Light is important for tree growth. That's why we built a shading model that simulates shades of trees on the ground.
We want our Model to be accessible and easy to use, especially for scientists. Our model was built in NetLogo (a modelling computer program) and we transfered it to NetLogoWeb, a tool that enables you to run our model inside your browser without installing anything.
We introduced our model in a paper that you can find HERE.
We share the data that was used to initialize the model and the results of our simulations in the DATA section.
We analyzed the simulations with the statical software R. You can find our calculations in the DATA ANALYSIS on this site.
